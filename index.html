Navratri Fasting Timetable

Satvik · 9 Sacred Days · Full & Energized

4 meals a day — Morning fuel · Heavy midday snack · Afternoon snack · Full satvik dinner.
Evening meals rotate: Roti sabzi · Satvik fried rice · Dal rice · Poori sabzi — all without onion & garlic.
💧 Daily: Drink 8–10 glasses of water or coconut water. Prevents fatigue, headaches & keeps hunger in check.
01

Shailputri — The Beginning

Evening: Roti + Aloo Sabzi + Dal

🌸 Goddess
Shailputri
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Warm Milk + Fruit Plate + Makhana

  • 🥛 1 big glass warm milk (mishri + cardamom)
  • 🍌 2 bananas
  • 🍎 1 apple
  • 🍿 1 cup roasted makhana (ghee + pepper)
  • 🌰 6 soaked almonds

Heavy breakfast — sets you up for 4–5 hours. Don't skip the makhana, it keeps you full longer than fruit alone.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Aloo Chaat + Yogurt + Peanuts

  • 🥔 2 boiled potatoes with ghee, jeera, sendha namak, lemon
  • 🥣 1 big bowl thick yogurt
  • 🥜 Large handful roasted peanuts

This is your main daytime fuel. Potatoes + yogurt + peanuts = carbs, protein, fat. Keeps you going till 4 PM easily.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Sweet Potato + Coconut Water + Fruit

  • 🍠 1 roasted sweet potato with ghee + sendha namak
  • 🥥 1 glass coconut water
  • 🍇 Small bowl grapes or seasonal fruit

Sweet potato is slow-digesting — perfect bridge before dinner. Coconut water replenishes electrolytes.

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal

Roti + Aloo Sabzi + Dal

  • 🫓 3–4 rotis (wheat or kuttu)
  • 🥔 Aloo sabzi (ghee, jeera, tomato, green chilli, sendha namak)
  • 🍲 Arhar dal (ghee tadka, jeera, sendha namak)
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita

Full proper dinner. Dal gives protein. Eat until well satisfied — this is your main satvik meal.

Day 1 Tip: Start strong — don't skip breakfast. The makhana + milk combo is the best anti-hunger combination for fasting days.
02

Brahmacharini — Discipline

Evening: Satvik Fried Rice

🌸 Goddess
Brahmacharini
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Banana Milkshake + Makhana + Fruits

  • 🥛 Thick banana milkshake (2 bananas + milk + mishri)
  • 🍿 1.5 cups makhana roasted in ghee
  • 🍈 1 bowl papaya or pomegranate
  • 🌰 6 soaked almonds + 3 walnuts

Smoothie is very filling. Makhana adds crunch. Nuts add healthy fat for brain focus during work hours.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Sweet Potato Tikki + Peanuts + Yogurt

  • 🍠 3–4 sweet potato tikkis (mashed, ghee pan-roasted, sendha namak)
  • 🥜 Large handful roasted peanuts
  • 🥣 1 big bowl thick yogurt

Tikkis are filling and satisfying. Make a batch in the morning — they reheat well at noon.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Makhana + Banana + Coconut Water

  • 🍿 1 cup makhana (ghee roasted)
  • 🍌 1–2 bananas
  • 🥥 1 glass coconut water

Quick and light. Coconut water replenishes electrolytes — great if you feel sluggish by 4 PM.

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal

Satvik Fried Rice + Aloo Sabzi + Raita

  • 🍚 Satvik fried rice (ghee, jeera, peas, capsicum, carrots, sendha namak — no onion/garlic)
  • 🥔 Aloo sabzi (tomato, ghee, green chilli)
  • 🥣 Cucumber yogurt raita

Fried rice feels like a treat! Use ghee + jeera generously for flavour. Load it with veggies.

Day 2 Tip: Coconut water is the secret weapon — it replaces electrolytes and kills the afternoon energy dip that hits around 3–4 PM during fasting.
03

Chandraghanta — Courage

Evening: Dal Rice + Aloo Sabzi

🌸 Goddess
Chandraghanta
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Haldi Milk + Spiced Potatoes + Makhana + Fruit

  • 🥛 1 glass warm haldi milk (turmeric + mishri + cardamom)
  • 🥔 Spiced boiled potatoes (ghee, jeera, sendha namak, green chilli)
  • 🍿 1 cup makhana
  • 🍎 1 apple + 🍌 1 banana

Spiced potatoes in the morning feel like aloo paratha filling! Very comforting and filling for a fasting breakfast.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Peanut Chaat + Yogurt + Fruit Bowl

  • 🥜 Large handful peanuts (sendha namak + lemon + jeera powder — toss together)
  • 🥣 1 big bowl yogurt (with honey or plain)
  • 🍈 1 bowl pomegranate or papaya

Peanut chaat with lemon + jeera tastes incredible and is very filling. Best midday snack combo for fasting.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Sweet Potato + Warm Milk

  • 🍠 1 boiled/roasted sweet potato with ghee
  • 🥛 1 small glass warm milk with cardamom

Sweet + warm — very satisfying in the late afternoon. Keeps you calm and not ravenous by dinner time.

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal

Dal Rice + Ghee + Aloo Sabzi + Papad

  • 🍚 Steamed rice (generous serving)
  • 🍲 Arhar dal (ghee tadka, jeera, tomato, sendha namak)
  • 🥔 Aloo sabzi on the side
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita
  • 🫓 Roasted papad

Dal chawal with ghee is ultimate comfort. Pour ghee on the rice generously — you need the healthy fat during fasting!

Day 3 Tip: If you feel a headache, add a pinch of sendha namak to lemon water and drink immediately — works like magic. Body is adjusting by Day 3.
04

Kushmanda — Energy

Evening: Poori + Aloo Sabzi + Kala Chana

🌸 Goddess
Kushmanda
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Fruit Bowl + Thick Yogurt + Makhana

  • 🍓 Mixed fruit bowl (banana, grapes, orange, apple)
  • 🥣 1 big bowl thick yogurt with honey
  • 🍿 1 cup makhana
  • 🌰 6 almonds + 3 walnuts (soaked)

Colourful fruits give antioxidants and natural sugar for morning energy. Yogurt protein keeps you full.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Aloo + Sweet Potato Chaat + Peanuts

  • 🥔 2 boiled potatoes + 🍠 1 sweet potato (chopped, ghee, lemon, sendha namak, jeera)
  • 🥜 Roasted peanuts mixed in
  • 🥣 1 bowl yogurt on the side

Mix potato + sweet potato together — best fasting chaat ever. Very hearty and fills you up properly.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Makhana Kheer + Banana

  • 🍿 Small bowl makhana kheer (milk + makhana + mishri + cardamom)
  • 🍌 1 banana if still hungry

A small kheer treat in the afternoon feels festive! Don't have too much — save appetite for poori night.

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal 🎉

Poori + Aloo Sabzi + Kala Chana

  • 🫓 5–6 pooris (wheat or kuttu, fried in ghee/oil)
  • 🥔 Spicy aloo sabzi (ghee, tomato, green chilli, jeera, sendha namak)
  • 🫘 Kala chana (boiled, ghee tadka, sendha namak)
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita

Poori night is everyone's favourite! Kala chana adds excellent protein. Eat well — you deserve it!

Day 4 Tip: Kala chana is a protein powerhouse during fasting — eat it whenever it appears in the menu. It prevents the muscle weakness some people feel while fasting.
05

Skandamata — Nourishment

Evening: Roti + Paneer Sabzi + Dal

🌸 Goddess
Skandamata
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Power Smoothie + Makhana

  • 🥛 Thick smoothie (milk + 2 bananas + pomegranate seeds + honey)
  • 🍿 1.5 cups makhana (ghee roasted, pepper + sendha namak)
  • 🌰 8 soaked almonds

Pomegranate adds iron which fights fasting fatigue — especially important at Day 5 midpoint.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Aloo Sabzi (Full Plate) + Yogurt + Peanuts

  • 🥔 Full generous plate of aloo sabzi with ghee
  • 🥣 1 big bowl yogurt
  • 🥜 Large handful roasted peanuts

Treat the midday snack like a proper meal — you need the calories to stay active and focused through the afternoon.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Sweet Potato + Apple + Coconut Water

  • 🍠 1 roasted sweet potato with ghee
  • 🍎 1 apple or pear
  • 🥥 Coconut water

Keep afternoon light — dinner tonight has rich paneer! Just enough to prevent pre-dinner hunger pangs.

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal

Roti + Paneer Sabzi + Moong Dal

  • 🫓 3–4 rotis
  • 🧀 Paneer sabzi (tomato gravy, ghee, jeera, green chilli — no onion/garlic)
  • 🍲 Moong dal (light, ghee tadka)
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita

Paneer on Day 5 gives excellent protein. Tomato-based gravy feels rich and full of flavour without onion-garlic.

Day 5 Tip: Halfway! Pomegranate in the morning and paneer at night make Day 5 the most nutritious day. Your body will feel it.
06

Katyayani — Strength

Evening: Satvik Fried Rice + Chana

🌸 Goddess
Katyayani
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Haldi Milk + Fruit Plate + Makhana + Nuts

  • 🥛 1 big glass warm haldi milk (turmeric + cardamom + mishri)
  • 🍌 2 bananas + 🍇 1 bowl grapes
  • 🍿 1 cup makhana
  • 🌰 6 almonds + 4 walnuts (soaked)

Haldi milk gives anti-inflammatory boost — you'll feel lighter and more energized throughout Day 6.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Sweet Potato Tikki + Peanut Chaat + Yogurt

  • 🍠 3–4 sweet potato tikkis (ghee pan-roasted)
  • 🥜 Peanut chaat (lemon + sendha namak + jeera powder)
  • 🥣 1 big bowl thick yogurt

This combination is incredibly filling. Make tikkis ahead of time — they're perfect for the midday meal.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Makhana + Apple + Coconut Water

  • 🍿 1 cup makhana
  • 🍎 1 apple
  • 🥥 1 glass coconut water

Don't oversnack at 4 PM — save good appetite for the fried rice dinner tonight!

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal

Satvik Fried Rice + Aloo Sabzi + Kala Chana

  • 🍚 Satvik fried rice (ghee, jeera, peas, capsicum, carrots, corn, sendha namak)
  • 🥔 Aloo sabzi (spicy, with tomato and ghee)
  • 🫘 Kala chana (boiled, ghee tadka)
  • 🥣 Cucumber raita

Veggie-loaded fried rice is incredibly satisfying. Kala chana on the side adds protein to this meal.

Day 6 Tip: Three days left — home stretch! If energy dips, eat an extra banana immediately. Bananas are the ultimate quick fasting energy fix.
07

Kalaratri — Fearlessness

Evening: Dal Rice + Sabzi

🌸 Goddess
Kalaratri
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Banana Smoothie + Makhana + Papaya

  • 🥛 Thick smoothie (milk + 2 bananas + honey + cardamom)
  • 🍿 1.5 cups makhana (ghee roasted)
  • 🍈 1 bowl papaya
  • 🌰 6 soaked almonds

Papaya on Day 7 aids digestion and keeps the gut healthy through extended fasting. Very good choice now.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Aloo + Makhana Bowl + Peanuts + Yogurt

  • 🥔 2 boiled potatoes with ghee, sendha namak, jeera
  • 🍿 ½ cup makhana mixed in
  • 🥜 Roasted peanuts
  • 🥣 1 big bowl yogurt

Mix everything into a single hearty bowl — very filling combination that keeps you going through the afternoon.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Sweet Potato + Warm Milk

  • 🍠 1 sweet potato (roasted, ghee + sendha namak)
  • 🥛 1 glass warm milk with mishri

Sweet and warm — perfect afternoon comfort. Milk here means you won't feel weak before dinner.

Dinner7:30 PMMain Meal

Dal Rice + Ghee + Lauki Sabzi + Papad

  • 🍚 Steamed basmati rice (big serving)
  • 🍲 Arhar dal (ghee tadka, jeera, tomato, sendha namak)
  • 🥗 Lauki / bottle gourd sabzi (ghee, jeera — light and digestive)
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita
  • 🫓 Roasted papad

Lauki sabzi is very gentle on the stomach — good for Day 7 when you've been fasting a while. Papad adds that satisfying crunch.

Day 7 Tip: Just 2 more days! Your body is fully adapted to the fasting rhythm now. Trust the routine you've built over these 7 days.
08

Mahagauri — Ashtami 🙏

Evening: Poori + Halwa + Kala Chana (Prasad)

🌸 Goddess
Mahagauri
Morning7:30 AMBreakfast

Warm Milk + Fruits + Makhana

  • 🌹 Begin with puja / aarti first
  • 🥛 1 glass warm milk (cardamom + mishri)
  • 🍌 2 bananas + 🍎 1 apple
  • 🍿 1 cup makhana
  • 🌰 6 soaked almonds

Ashtami is most sacred — do puja first, eat after. Keep the morning simple and clean before the day's rituals.

Midday12:30 PMHeavy Snack

Aloo Chaat + Peanuts + Yogurt

  • 🥔 Spiced boiled potatoes (big serving — ghee, lemon, jeera, sendha namak)
  • 🥜 Roasted peanuts
  • 🥣 1 big bowl yogurt

Keep yourself well fed before Kanjak puja rituals. Don't go into afternoon prayers on an empty stomach.

Afternoon4:00 PMSnack

Prasad Taste + Coconut Water + Banana

  • 🍯 Small taste of halwa while preparing prasad
  • 🥥 Coconut water
  • 🍌 1 banana

A small nibble while making prasad is absolutely part of the ritual joy — enjoy it! 😊

Dinner7:30 PMCelebration Meal 🎉

Kanjak Thali — Poori + Halwa + Kala Chana

  • 🫓 5–6 pooris (wheat, fried in ghee)
  • 🍯 Suji halwa (ghee + sugar + cardamom)
  • 🫘 Kala chana (boiled, ghee tadka)
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita

This is the celebration meal! Eat joyfully — but the halwa is very rich, so keep that portion small and enjoy it slowly.

Day 8 Tip: Ashtami is the most festive day — enjoy every bit of the prasad thali. You've earned it after 8 days of dedication. Jai Mata Di! 🙏

Siddhidatri

Celebration Fruit Plate + Smoothie

  • 🥛 Thick smoothie (milk + banana + honey)
  • 🍿 1 cup makhana
  • 🌰 All your nuts — almonds + walnuts, soaked

Last day! Make breakfast beautiful — you've completed 9 days. Celebrate every single meal today.

Aloo + Sweet Potato Chaat + Peanuts + Yogurt

  • 🥔 Potato + 🍠 sweet potato chaat (mixed, ghee, lemon, jeera)
  • 🥜 Roasted peanuts

Makhana Kheer + Haldi Milk

Turmeric milk on the final day is a beautiful tradition — anti-inflammatory and deeply nourishing. A perfect closing ritual.

Dinner7:30 PMGrand Finale 🏆

Grand Satvik Feast — Everything!

  • 🫓 Poori OR roti (your choice)
  • 🥔 Aloo sabzi
  • 🍲 Dal
  • 🍚 Rice
  • 🧀 Paneer sabzi
  • 🫘 Kala chana
  • 🍯 Halwa or makhana kheer (dessert)
  • 🥣 Yogurt raita

9 days complete! 🙏 Eat with joy but mindfully — your gut is cleansed. Tomorrow reintroduce regular food slowly, not all at once.

Day 9 Tip: After the fast, go slow for 1–2 days before jumping back to spicy or heavy food. Your digestion is clean and sensitive — honour that.
